Regex Patterns:
    Elongated Affirmations:
        yes{2,} matches "yess", "yesss", etc.
        y{2,} matches "yy", "yyy", etc.
        sure{2,} matches "suree", "sureee", etc.
    Elongated Rejections:
        no{2,} matches "noo", "nooo", etc.
        never{2,} matches "neverr", "neverrr", etc.
        dont{2,} matches "dontt", "donttt", etc.
